Customizable Soup Recipe

Description

This soup is as nutritious as you decide to make it. You can fill it with a variety of vegetables, meat, and noodles or rice. It is truly a no-fuss soup that gives the satisfaction of eating a home-cooked meal.

Ingredients

  • tofu
  • soba noodles
  • broccoli
  • mini cucumbers
  • king oyster mushrooms
  • cabbage
  • 1 egg
  • water
  • 1 tbsp bone broth powder

Steps

  1. Fill your pot up with water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the bone broth and mix until dissolved in the water.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and let boil until cooked. You may cover the pot with a lid if you wish to.
