Japanese Potato Salad Recipe

Description

Japanese-style potato salad is special in the veggies and eggy-tasting mayo used to make it. A satisfying crunch accompanies each creamy bite. This is the perfect side dish to complete any meal.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • cucumbers
  • eggs
  • Kewpie mayo
  • salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into cubes. Cut carrots and cucumbers into thin strips.
  2. Boil potatoes, carrots, cucumbers, and eggs in a pot with water.
  3. Mash the potatoes in a big bowl.
  4. Peel the eggs and dice them.
  5. Add the carrots, cucumbers, and eggs to the same bowl.
  6. Add Kewpie mayo and salt (if needed) to the mix and stir until all ingredients are combined well.
